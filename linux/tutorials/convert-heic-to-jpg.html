Convert .heic to .jpg

Prerequisites

  • ImageMagick installed
  • Magick installed

Procedure

Convert Single Image

Execute the following command in the directory where the heic image is found

convert {filename.heic} {filename.jpg}

or

magick {filename.heic} {filename.jpg}
