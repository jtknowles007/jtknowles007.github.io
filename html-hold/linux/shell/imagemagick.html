Purpose

ImageMagick  is used for editing and manipulating digital images.  It can be used to create, edit, compose, or convert bitmap images, and supports a wide range of file formats, including JPEG, PNG, GIF, TIFF, and PDF.  ImageMagick includes a command-line interface for executing complex image processing tasks.
