Chicken Enchelada Casserole

Description

PREP TIME: 20 minutes
COOK TIME: 45 minutes
SERVINGS: 6

Ingredients

1 pound chicken breast
20oz Enchelada sauce
6 medium corn tortillas OR tortilla chips
3 cups shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Cut each chicken breast into 3 pieces and place in small pot. Pour enchelada sauce over the chicken and cook, coveredd, on medium heat until chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
  2. Remove chicken from the pot and shred with two forks.
  3. PReheat overn to 375degrees.
  4. Start layering casserole. Start with about 1/4 cup of leftover sauce in the bottom of the baking dish. Place tortillas or tortilla chips on bottom, top with 1/3 of the chicken and 1/3 of sauce. Sprinkle with 1/3 of cheese. Repeat two more times.
  5. Bake for 20-30 minutes, uncovered, until bubbly and cheese has melted and started to brown on top.
  6. Serve warm.
