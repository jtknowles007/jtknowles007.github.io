Grill “Baked” Potato

Description

Ingredients

1 medium russet potato 1 pat butter 1 pinch salt

Directions

  1. Rub potato with butter and sprinkle with salt
  2. Wrap in foil and cook on grill with lid down for 50 minutes
  3. Turn after 25 minutes (No need to turn if smoked)
  4. Squeeze to check for doneness
